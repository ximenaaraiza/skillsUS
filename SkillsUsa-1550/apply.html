Food Booth Rental Agreement Guidelines

Reservations

  • Deadline: September 2, 202. Applications after this date incur a $25.00 late fee (Sept. 5 – Sept. 8). No applications accepted after Sept. 9.
  • Preference: Local non-profit organizations prioritized if applied by the deadline.
  • Allocation: First come, first serve basis.

Location & Setup

  • Location Rights: Festival Committee reserves the right to assign concession locations.
  • Setup Time: Begin at 8:00 a.m. on Sept. 27. Operation required by 4:00 p.m. Friday and 10:00 a.m. Saturd4ay.
  • Vehicle Access: Limited times for vehicle access in food booth areas. One reserved parking space per booth.

Fees

  • County Non-Profit/Chamber Members: $175.00 (single space), $300.00 (double space)
  • General Retail: $200.00 (single space), $350.00 (double space)
  • Environmental Health Certificate: $91.00
  • Fire Department Safety Inspection: $20.00
  • Liability Insurance (Provided by Chamber): $75.00
  • Cleaning Deposit (Non-members): $100.00

Space Details

  • Single Space: 10’ x 20’ area with one parking space.
  • Double Space: 20’ x 20’ area with two parking permits.
